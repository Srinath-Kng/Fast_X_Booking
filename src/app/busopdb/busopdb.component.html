Bus Operator Dashboard

Welcome to the Bus Operator Dashboard

This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of bus operations, including managing buses, bookings, and amenities. The tools available will help streamline your daily tasks and provide valuable insights into your operations.

Manage Buses

Easily add, edit, and remove buses from your fleet. Ensure all bus details are accurate and up-to-date for smooth operations.

Bookings Overview

View and manage all bookings made for your buses. Keep track of seat availability and ensure a smooth booking experience.

Amenities Management

Update and manage the amenities available on your buses. Offer passengers the best possible experience by providing relevant amenities.
